About Us


Welcome to The Cosmic Restaurant

The Cosmic Restaurant is a vibrant culinary gem, offering an authentic taste of traditional Indian cuisine in a warm, rustic setting. Nestled conveniently along Gauhati University, this restaurant serves as a beloved pit stop for travelers and localites alike, seeking hearty meals and a welcoming atmosphere.

Renowned for its rich, flavorful dishes, including aromatic biryanis, succulent tandoori delights, and a variety of vegetarian options. The Cosmic Restaurant ensures a memorable dining experience with its meticulous atttention to culinary excellence. Complemented by friendly serving and an inviting ambiance, it stands out as a must-visit destination for anyone craving genuine Indian flavors.
